⭐ The golden rule that separates good LLD candidates from average ones
Don't think in terms of classes first. Think in terms of responsibilities.

Bad approach:
"I'll make a Ride class and put everything in it."

Good approach:
"What is the responsibility of each part of the system?"
Once the responsibilities are clear, the classes, interfaces, SOLID principles, and design patterns almost reveal themselves naturally.
This is the same process you can use for Parking Lot, Food Delivery, Hotel Booking, Library Management, Movie Ticket Booking, and Ride Sharing. Once you practice it a few times, you'll be able to sketch an LLD design in 5–10 minutes before writing any code.
