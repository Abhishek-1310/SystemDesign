Strategy pattern
Definition
The Strategy Pattern allows you to define a family of algorithms, put each one into its own class, and make them 
interchangeable at runtime.
In simple words:
Instead of writing lots of if-else statements, we move each behavior into its own class and choose which one to use at runtime.

Our Problem
We have a payment system.
A customer can pay using (so many option/Strategy)

Credit Card
PayPal
UPI
Bitcoin (future)

Motivation
Let's consider a simple payment system where users can pay using different methods like credit cards or PayPal. Without the 
Strategy Pattern, you might use if-else conditions to handle the different payment methods, leading to less maintainable and
 flexible code.
